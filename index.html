Our Story

A Taste of
the Arabian Gulf
in Kerala

Nestled on Thodupuzha's vibrant River View Food Street, Al Maraa brings the authentic warmth and bold flavors of Gulf cuisine to the heart of Idukki. Every dish is crafted with time-honored recipes, fragrant spices, and a genuine love for Arabian hospitality.

From the slow-smoked Mandi to perfectly charred Shawarma, we honor traditions passed down through generations — served fresh, every evening, for our cherished guests.
